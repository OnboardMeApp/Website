OnboardMe + Pinch Payments
An Integration built for Real Service Businesses

Onboarding a new client should feel like a win, not a whirlwind of admin. That’s why OnboardMe has partnered with Pinch Payments to combine engagement creation, automated billing, and secure payment collection into a single, powerful flow. This integration is designed for how real businesses work – efficiently, professionally, and with cash-flow in mind.

What This Integration Delivers

  • Integrated Payment Capture – credit card & bank debit in one flow
  • Get Paid On Acceptance – turn proposal sign-off into instant payment
  • Auto-Reconciliation straight into your accounting software
  • Recurring & Upfront Billing – payment plans, split invoices, or bill in full
  • Branded Client Experience – your logo, colours & terms with a secure interface
  • Smart Automation – real-time updates and flexible follow-ups for missed payments
